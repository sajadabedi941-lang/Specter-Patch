ADVANCED AIR BASE REPORT
Specter-Patch | branch cursor/advanced-airbase-finalize-f792
================================================================================

SUMMARY
-------
Advanced Air Base replaces Strategy Center as the strategic aviation / doctrine
facility. Normal Airfield CommandSets stay stock (fighters/helicopters unchanged).
Large aircraft (B-2, B-52, Tu-160, H-6, AWACS, Tanker, Transport, Tu-22, B-1R)
build at Advanced Air Base by faction identity. No aircraft removed.
sync_audit.py PASS.


1. STRATEGY CENTER REMOVAL
--------------------------
Player dozers no longer build Strategy Center:
  AmericaDozerCommandSet, NatoDozerCommandSet, AirF_AmericaDozerCommandSet
  — slot 2 = *_AdvancedAirBase (not Strategy Center).

Russia / China / Iran dozers: Advanced Air Base added (no Strategy Center role).

All Strategy Center CommandSets (America / Nato / AirF / SupW / Lazr / tiers):
  Sell-only — legacy buildings cannot produce upgrades/plans.


2. ADVANCED AIR BASE BUILDING
-----------------------------
Object IDs (*_AdvancedAirBase):
  America, Russia, China, Iran, Iraq, Nato, GLA, AirF_America,
  Turkey, Ukraine, Pakistan, SaudiArabia, UAE, India, Japan, NorthKorea.

DisplayName: OBJECT:Patch_AdvancedAirBase
ParkingPlaceBehavior:
  NumRows=2 NumCols=6 → 12 pads (vs normal airfield ~1×4)
  HealAmountPerSecond=28 (repair / reload support)
  HasRunways=Yes, ApproachHeight=100
BattlePlanUpdate: ModuleTag_PatchBattlePlans (plans activate at AAB)
PatchBaseCost class ~4200 (CountryBalance baked per Side)

Files:
  PatchSystems/AdvancedAirBase_AllFactions.ini
  PatchSystems/AdvancedAirBase_FutureFactions.ini
  Turkey Armed Forces/Buildings/Turkey_AdvancedAirBase.ini

6. AIRCRAFT SPAWN / CAPACITY / AI
---------------------------------
Spawn: ParkingPlaceBehavior 2×6 runway bones (US_AirField model family).
Capacity: 12 simultaneous parked aircraft vs ~4 on normal airfield.
Repair/reload: HealAmountPerSecond=28.
AI: shared player dozer CS builds AAB instead of Strategy Center; Sell-only SC
CommandSets prevent useful SC production if AI places a leftover SC.


7. FACTION COMPATIBILITY
------------------------

Future-faction construct buttons authored for Ukraine/Pakistan/Saudi/UAE/
India/Japan/NorthKorea (wire to faction dozers when those CS are completed).
Missile TELs on some AAB slots are additive and do not remove aircraft.
