ADVANCED AIR BASE REPORT
Specter-Patch | branch cursor/advanced-airbase-system-f792
================================================================================

SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Advanced Air Base replaces Strategy Center as the strategic aviation / doctrine
facility for all factions. Normal Airfield CommandSets are left stock (fighters
and helicopters unchanged). Large aircraft are produced at Advanced Air Base
only as faction-identity additions. sync_audit PASS required.


1. STRATEGY CENTER REMOVAL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Player dozers (America / Nato / AirF Israel) no longer build Strategy Center.
Slot replaced with Command_Construct*_AdvancedAirBase.
Strategy Center CommandSets are Sell-only (legacy buildings / AI leftovers).


2. ADVANCED AIR BASE BUILDING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Object IDs: *_AdvancedAirBase (America, Russia, China, Iran, Iraq, Nato, GLA,
AirF, Turkey, Ukraine, Pakistan, Saudi, UAE, India, Japan, NorthKorea).

DisplayName: OBJECT:Patch_AdvancedAirBase
Parking: 2 rows × 6 cols = 12 pads (normal Turkey Airfield_T = 1×4)
HealAmountPerSecond: 28 (repair/refuel vs ~10–11 on normal airfield)
ApproachHeight: 100 | larger Geometry footprint
PatchBaseCost: 4200 class (CountryBalance baked per Side)
BattlePlanUpdate: ModuleTag_PatchBattlePlans (plans activate at AAB)


3. FACTION AIRCRAFT RESTRICTIONS (AAB CommandSets)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Faction     Advanced Air Base roster
----------  --------------------------------------------------------------
USA         B-1R + battle plans (no Turkey B-2/B-52)
Russia      Tu-22M3M + Tu-160 (new Russia_Tu160) — no USA bombers
Turkey      B-2, B-52, AWACS, Tanker, Transport, Tu-22M3 + plans/upgrades
Nato        Battle plans only (no US strategic bombers)
Israel/AirF Battle plans shell
China/Iran/Iraq/GLA/others  Pads + rally/sell until faction heavies authored

Normal Airfield: NOT overridden — all existing fighters/helos/drones remain.


4. GENERAL POWER / STAR SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Battle doctrines (Rank1 PurchaseScience):
  America / Nato / Turkey / AirF — Bombardment, Hold the Line, Search and Destroy
Former America Strategy Center upgrades → Rank3 sciences:
  Chemical Suits, Composite Armor, Advanced Training, Supply Lines
Activation of plans: Command_InitiateBattlePlan* on Advanced Air Base.


5. NEW / UPDATED CONTENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Object Russia_Tu160 (PatchSystems/HeavyAircraft) — Side=Russia, MaxSim=1,
  Prereq Russia_AdvancedAirBase, high PatchBaseCost
Command_ConstructRussia_Tu160 button
CommandSet_AdvancedAirBase.ini (dozers, AAB CS, science ranks)
CommandButton_AdvancedAirBase_SpecterFactions.ini + Strategic doctrine buttons


6. PREREQUISITES / COSTS / AI
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Heavy aircraft require faction *_AdvancedAirBase.
Akinci uses Turkey_AdvancedAirBase (not AmericaStrategyCenter).
AI: player-shared dozer CS builds AAB instead of Strategy Center; empty SC CS
prevents useful SC production if AI places a leftover SC.
