SUMMARY
================================================================================
Audited previously added aircraft/weapons (B-2, B-52, drones, fighters, bombers,
helicopters, missile/AD units) under patch/ only.

Critical issues found and repaired:
  1. B-2 / B-52 were buildable by Turkey and North Korea (unrealistic).
  2. America had no player-buildable B-2 / B-52 (AAB used stock CS without heavies).
  3. Turkey unique air had wrong Prerequisites (America/Nato buildings / missing sciences).
  4. North Korea player production was a full Turkish kit clone (identity violation).
  5. Several Turkish AD/missile clones were on NK WarFactory/MIC CommandSets.
