GENERAL STAR SYSTEM REPORT
Specter-Patch | branch cursor/faction-tech-general-stars-f792
================================================================================

SUMMARY
-------
Unique per-country General Star abilities and tech doctrines added with
balanced purchase costs and long ability cooldowns. No unlimited spam powers.
Existing Star CommandSets extended additively where already owned by patch.
sync_audit.py PASS (errors=0).


1. DOCTRINES (Rank1)
--------------------
Battle-plan doctrines for Russia, China, Iran, Ukraine, Pakistan, SaudiArabia,
UAE, India, Japan, NorthKorea (America/Nato/Turkey already had doctrines).
Purchase cost: 1 point each.


2. TECH DOCTRINES (Rank3)
-------------------------
SCIENCE_<Side>_Tech_* — 1–2 points. Mirrors faction upgrade themes.
America Rank3: + Stealth / Precision / Advanced Aircraft
Turkey Rank3: + UAV / Fighter / Modern AD
Other nations: national SCIENCE_<Side>_CommandSetRank3

4. BALANCE RULES
----------------
- High SciencePurchasePointCost (2–3) for late abilities
- Reload 220–400 seconds
- No zero-cooldown supers
- Strategic upgrades also available as expensive PLAYER_UPGRADE research


5. STRATEGY CENTER → STARS
--------------------------

on America Rank3. AAB keeps activation of battle plans + Countermeasures
research. New tech themes prefer Stars (Rank3) with optional AAB/MIC research.
