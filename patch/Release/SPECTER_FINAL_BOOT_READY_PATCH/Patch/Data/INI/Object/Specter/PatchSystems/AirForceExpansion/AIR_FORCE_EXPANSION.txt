SPECTER PATCH — Air Force Expansion
====================================
Overlay-only integration of audited mod.zip air roles into Patch_* IDs.

Rules
-----
- Never overwrite Specter/vendor archives or stock Object IDs.
- Unique Patch_* / Russia_AD_TorM IDs only.
- Specter art models (US_/RU_/CN_/Irq_) — no dependency on incomplete mod.z23/z24 W3D.
- Heavy scales: bomber 0.72, AWACS 0.78, transport/gunship 0.76, heavy helo 0.82.
- Fixed weapon timings for multiplayer sync.
- NO NAVAL (aircarrier / corvette content audited but not imported).

AAB wiring
----------
Expansion airframes are slotted into existing *_AdvancedAirBaseCommandSet sets
(CommandSet_AdvancedAirBase.ini) without removing prior Turkey/AAB entries.
