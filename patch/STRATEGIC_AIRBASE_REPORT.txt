STRATEGIC AIRBASE REPORT
Specter-Patch | branch cursor/strategic-airbase-system-f792
================================================================================

SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Strategy Center is removed from player build paths. Its role is replaced by the
Strategic Air Base (existing *_AdvancedAirBase Objects, rebranded). Battle plans
and former Strategy Center upgrades move into General Stars / faction research.
No fighters, bombers, helicopters, or custom aircraft Objects were deleted.


1. STRATEGY CENTER REMOVAL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Player dozers no longer construct Strategy Center:
  AmericaDozerCommandSet     — SC slots → America_AdvancedAirBase (Strategic Air Base)
  NatoDozerCommandSet        — NatoStrategyCenter → Nato_AdvancedAirBase
  AirF_AmericaDozerCommandSet — AirF StrategyCenter → AirF_AmericaAdvancedAirBase

Strategy Center CommandSets reduced to Sell-only (legacy/AI buildings if present):
  AmericaStrategyCenterCommandSet (+_T/_T1/_T2), AirF_*, Nato*, SupW_*, Lazr_*


2. STRATEGIC AIR BASE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Object IDs kept as *_AdvancedAirBase (compatibility). Presentation:
  DisplayName = OBJECT:Patch_StrategicAirBase
  Construct buttons use CONTROLBAR:ConstructPatch_StrategicAirBase

Facility upgrades:
  Parking 1×6 → 2×4 pads (8) | ApproachHeight 90 | larger Geometry (140×95×30)
  PatchBaseCost raised toward 4200 (strategic building)
  BattlePlanUpdate ModuleTag_PatchBattlePlans added (plans activate at SAB)

CommandSets (*_StrategicAirBaseCommandSet):
  Turkey: B-2, B-52, AWACS, Tanker, Transport, Tu-22M3 + battle plans + upgrades
  America: B-1R + battle plans (fighters remain on Airfield)
  Russia: Tu-22M3M (fighters/helos remain on Airfield; Tu-22 removed from airfield CS)
  Other factions: rally/sell shells until faction heavy content is authored


3. AIRFIELD VS STRATEGIC SPLIT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Normal Airfield: fighters, helicopters, drones (America B1R removed from airfield CS;
  Russia Tu-22 removed from airfield CS — aircraft still buildable at SAB).
Strategic Air Base: large/strategic aircraft only.


4. GENERAL STARS / RESEARCH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Battle doctrines (PurchaseScience on Rank1):
  America: SCIENCE_PatchDoctrine{Bombardment,HoldTheLine,SearchAndDestroy}
  Turkey:  SCIENCE_TurkeyDoctrine*
  Nato:    SCIENCE_NatoDoctrine*

Former America Strategy Center upgrades → Rank3 sciences:
  ChemicalSuits, CompositeArmor, AdvancedTraining, SupplyLines

Activation of battle plans remains on Strategic Air Base CommandSet
(Command_InitiateBattlePlan*) after doctrines are researched.


5. PREREQUISITES / COSTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Heavy aircraft require Turkey_AdvancedAirBase (Strategic Air Base Object).
Akinci no longer requires AmericaStrategyCenter.
B-2 / B-52 PatchBaseCost raised (larger strategic platforms, MaxSim=1).


6. FILES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CommandSet_StrategicAirBase.ini
CommandButton_StrategicAirBase.ini
CommandButton_AdvancedAirBase_SpecterFactions.ini (labels)
Science_StrategicDoctrines.ini
Object/.../AdvancedAirBase_*.ini + Turkey_AdvancedAirBase.ini
CommandSet_Turkey.ini / CommandSet_SpecterPatch.ini / CommandSet_Turkey_AirSlots.ini


7. AI
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Player dozers no longer offer Strategy Center. AI-only THAAD construct removed from
patched America/Nato/AirF dozer sets. AI that still owns a Strategy Center object
can only Sell it (empty CS). Prefer Strategic Air Base for new AI build orders.
