STRATEGIC BOMBER BALANCE REPORT
Specter-Patch | branch cursor/strategic-bomber-balance-f792
================================================================================

SUMMARY
-------
Strategic bombers are split into two pools:
  Patch_StrategicBomberNative (MaxSim=3) — USA / Russia / China native bombers
  Patch_StrategicBomber       (MaxSim=1) — all other factions' limited heavy access

No aircraft were removed. Faction identity rosters are preserved (Turkey keeps
B-2 / B-52 / Tu-22M3; Russia keeps Tu-22M3M + Tu-160; USA keeps stock B-1R;
China gains native H-6 at Advanced Air Base).

RULES APPLIED
-------------
USA / Russia / China
  - Native strategic bombers only (no cross-faction USA B-2/B-52 handout)
  - Normal purchase cost and availability
  - Shared native pool MaxSimultaneous = 3 (Patch_StrategicBomberNative)
  - Produced at Advanced Air Base

Other factions (Turkey implemented; contract for future faction heavies)
  - Limited heavy bomber access kept (identity roster unchanged)
  - Very high purchase cost (~8800–10200 after CountryBalance)
  - Maximum 1 active bomber per faction (shared LinkKey across B2/B52/Tu-22)
  - Normal heavy build time (~28–32s), not extreme
  - Require Advanced Air Base
  - Require high technology (SCIENCE_Rank3)

SCIENCE / TECH
--------------
Limited bombers require SCIENCE_Rank3 (high technology). AI ranks unlock this
naturally (AI-compatible). Native USA/RU/CN bombers do not require Rank3.
